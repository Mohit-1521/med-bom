About Mediation Bombay 6.0

Mediation Bombay 6.0 is a celebration of excellence and innovation in dispute resolution. Over five successful editions, it has grown into India’s premier mediation competition and remains the only one in the country to feature a unique tripartite finals format, simulating real-world disputes between parties, counsels, and mediators.

With international participation, including back-to-back wins by Singapore Management University, the competition serves as a bridge between Asian legal talent and the global ADR community. Hosted in Mumbai, Mediation Bombay continues to be a leading platform for aspiring mediators and negotiators worldwide.

Our Vision

At Mediation Bombay, we aim to nurture, foster, and enhance the growth and quality of Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) practices in India. As young lawyers, it is rare to encounter a professional environment that genuinely embraces mediation with an open and accommodating mindset. Here, we strive to change the notion and perception of mediation. We seek to emphasize that speaking up is not interference, sharing perspectives isn't bias, and a mediator is far more than just a mere recorder or facilitator.

About our university

Founded in 2014, Maharashtra National Law University Mumbai is one of India’s youngest yet most dynamic National Law Universities. Beyond academic excellence and rapid recognition, the University is defined by its strong sense of community and its belief that law students are not just future lawyers, but changemakers, thinkers, and leaders.

With a diverse student body, forward-looking faculty, and initiatives that extend beyond the classroom, MNLU Mumbai fosters innovation, individuality, and continuous learning. Consistently ranked among the top NLUs and one of the fastest-growing institutions in the country, the University is guided by the esteemed Chancellor, Justice Alok Aradhe, and Vice-Chancellor Prof. (Dr.) Dilip Ukey, whose leadership continues to strengthen its pursuit of excellence.

About the ADRS

The Alternative Dispute Resolution Society (ADRS) of Maharashtra National Law University Mumbai is a dynamic, student-led platform dedicated to honing skills in mediation, negotiation, and arbitration. Through flagship events like Mediation Bombay, along with novice, intra, and client counselling competitions, ADRS fosters a vibrant culture of dispute resolution on campus.

Recognised among the top four campus mediation societies in India at the Maadhyam-PACT National ADR Board Awards 2023, ADRS remains committed to expanding awareness and excellence in ADR through workshops and collaborations with institutions such as the Centre for Mediation and Research.
