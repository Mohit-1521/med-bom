“Meet the Team” Page

Faculty Convener - Tanaya Ma’am:

As the Faculty Convenor of the ADR Society and Mediation Bombay, I have had the privilege of witnessing the transformative impact of this initiative on our students. Mediation Bombay is not only a flagship event of our university but also a reflection of the values of dialogue, empathy, and problem-solving that we strive to instill. Each edition reaffirms my belief that young minds, when given the right platform, can push boundaries and set new standards in dispute resolution. It fills me with pride to see our students carry forward this vision with such passion and dedication.

Chairperson - Arin Agrawal:

Being the Chairperson of Mediation Bombay has been one of the most rewarding journeys of my university life. This event is so much more than a competition, it is a space where ideas meet, conversations flow, and lasting connections are built. Leading the team has shown me the power of collaboration, trust, and the shared belief that mediation can truly shape the way we resolve conflicts. Watching Mediation Bombay grow and inspire students from across the globe has been humbling, and I feel grateful to have been a part of this incredible journey.

Associate Chairperson – Vikhyat Sharma:

My journey as the Associate Chairperson of Mediation Bombay has been one of the most rewarding chapters of my life. Mediation Bombay is not just an event, it is one of the world’s premier international mediation competitions, a stage where the best of the best come together. To work behind the scenes of a forum that hosts world-class judges and welcomes exceptional teams from across the globe has been a privilege in itself. Along the way, I discovered the thrill of building something bigger than myself, and the joy of watching participants test their skills in front of the finest minds in mediation. Mediation Bombay has been a masterclass in teamwork, excellence, and perseverance, and I feel grateful to have played a part in shaping this incredible journey.

Head of Operations – Nazifa Khateeb:

Being the Head of Operations for Mediation Bombay and Convenor of the ADR Society has been one of the most fulfilling chapters of my university life. Behind the scenes, I saw the spirit, energy, and passion that makes MedBom so special. It was never just about operations it was about the people, the shared vision, and the bonds we built while making it all happen. Balancing responsibilities wasn’t always easy, but every moment was worth it. MedBom has given me lifelong lessons, dear friends, and the joy of watching an idea transform into something truly impactful.
